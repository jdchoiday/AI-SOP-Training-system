# SOP - BAR - HOW TO OPEN AND SERVE A BOTTLE OF WHITE WINE

> Nguồn gốc: `SOP-OPEN/SERVE WHITE WINE` — KIWOOZA · F&B · BAR · All staffs · Modified by: Amber Truong · 10/06/2020.
> Bản trích xuất TOÀN VĂN (EN). KHÔNG sửa file gốc.

---

## OBJECTIVE
Help Associates understand the **sequence of White Wine bottle service**.

---

## 1. Prepare a bottle of white wine
- **Check the wine label and bin number** against the POS printed order.
- Obtain the bottle and **put it in the wine bucket**; fill ice cubes to the rim and add **cold water**.
- Put **napkin over the wine cooler** from right to left, overhanging on the sides.

## 2. Present the white wine
- Present the bottle horizontal, label uppermost, service cloth supporting base & back:
  > *"Mr/Mrs/Ms/Sir/Madam XXXX, this is the wine you have ordered; may I open it now?"*

## 3. Open the wine
- Place the white wine into the wine bucket.
- Open the knife from the wine opener; ensure knife is **sharp, clean, not rusty**.

## 4. Cutting the foil
- Hold the bottle's neck firmly.
- Cut the foil **~0.5 cm down the mouth**, turn the knife **clockwise twice and anti-clockwise once**.
- Remove the cut foil, place it in your pocket.
- Close the knife carefully.

## 5. Pull the cork
- Pierce the cork in the middle with the opener's skewer; lift upward (right hand), left hand holding neck firmly.
- Place the cork upwards on the guest table beside the wine glass, or use a **cork holder** (if applicable) in front of the head of the table / the person who will taste.
- **Wipe the bottle's mouth inside out.**

## 6. Presenting the wine
- With the **label facing the guest**, pour a mouthful for the guest to taste (**approx. 5–10% of the glass**).
- After tasting, serve **clockwise, ladies first**, the person who tasted **last**.
