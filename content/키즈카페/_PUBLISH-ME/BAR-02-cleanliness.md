## OBJECTIVE
Associates must know that **cleanliness is one of the most important points of the bar**.

---

## CLEANING THE BAR
- Immediately after an order is prepared, use a **dry cloth/bussing towel** to wipe all wet areas.
- Replace bottles & items used in making the order to the **correct location**, wiping bottles if necessary.
- Always use a **clean cloth** when wiping areas in front of the guest at the counter.
- **Do not leave the bar service area wet and disorganized.**
- Put knives, strainers, cutting boards, etc. back to proper place, arranged orderly.
- **Clean all equipment after use.**
- Pay attention to: **drip trays for coffee machine, draught beer machine, bar-gun nozzle, cutting boards, pick-up areas, sinks**, etc.

## CLEANING THE FRIDGE
1. **Remove all items** from the fridge.
2. Use clean cloth to wipe up all spillage.
3. With **warm soapy water**, wipe shelf base, sides, door and steel.
4. **Dry out the shelf base** with a dry towel (ensures no water-borne bacteria survive).
5. Wipe seals with damp cloth, ensuring **all mold is removed**.
6. Replace all items back; **wipe and polish** thoroughly with clean towel.
