# SOP - BAR - CHECKING COOLING EQUIPMENT

## OBJECTIVE
Help Associates understand the importance of **checking all cooling equipment before service**.

---

## PROCEDURE
- At the **beginning of the shift**, all cooling areas should be inspected.
- Fridge maintained to a **standard temperature** so all fruits & beverages are chilled evenly.
- **White wine fridges/coolers: minimum 2–8ºC.** Do not over-chill wines.
- **Beverage fridges: always 2–8ºC.**
- If there is a **sharp decrease/increase** of temperature, **inform Engineering Department immediately**.
- In case of a **breakdown**, remove wine & champagne to another cooler — **don't wait** for the Engineer to repair.
- Beverage items (cans, bottles, etc.) **must never block fan blowers**.
- The fridge/cooler systems **must not be switched off** — it will result in damage.
- Vegetable/fruit must be **covered or wrapped with plastic cling film** before putting into the fridge.
- Always **close the fridge quickly** whenever you open it and ensure it is firmly shut.
