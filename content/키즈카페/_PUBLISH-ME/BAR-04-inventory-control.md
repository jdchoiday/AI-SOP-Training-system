## OBJECTIVE
Associates know how to do a proper **inventory control**.

---

## PROCEDURE
1. **All beverages must have a proper control.**
2. Service associates conduct **daily inventory**:
   - **Opening** = closing of the day before
   - **In – raw** = stocks bought in day
   - **Pre-processing** = stocks pre-processed (fresh fruit, mint)
   - **Amount of loss** = (in-raw) − (pre-processing) + cancel items + spoiled stocks
   - **Out – consuming** = stocks sold in day
   - **Closing** = opening + (pre-processing) − (amount of loss) − (out-consuming)
3. **Cost control department** conducts **weekly inventory**. Ensure all baristas follow **recipe cards**, use measuring cups and spoons.
4. **Order cancelling** must be reported to managers with reasons.
5. **Bar inventory:** checked every day.
6. **Kitchen inventory:**
   - **Daily** (the day after): main ingredients with portions.
   - **Weekly** (Monday): check all ingredients and stocks.
