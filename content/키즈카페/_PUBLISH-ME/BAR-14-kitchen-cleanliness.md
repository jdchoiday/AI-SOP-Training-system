# SOP - KITCHEN - CLEANLINESS OF THE KITCHEN

## OBJECTIVE
Associates must know that **cleanliness is one of the most important points of the kitchen**.

---

## CLEANING (the work area)
- Immediately after an order is prepared, use a **dry cloth/bussing towel** to wipe all wet areas.
- Replace bottles & items used in making the **food order** to the correct location, wiping if necessary.
- **Do not leave the area wet and disorganized.**
- Put knives, strainers, cutting boards, etc. back to proper place, arranged orderly.
- **Clean all equipment after use.**

## CLEANING THE FRIDGE
1. **Remove all items** from the fridge.
2. Use clean cloth to wipe up all spillage.
3. With **warm soapy water**, wipe shelf base, sides, door and steel.
4. **Dry out the shelf base** with a dry towel (no water-borne bacteria survive).
5. Wipe seals with damp cloth, ensuring **all mold is removed**.
6. Replace all items back; **wipe and polish** thoroughly with clean towel.
