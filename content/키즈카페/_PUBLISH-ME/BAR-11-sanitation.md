# SOP - BAR - SANITATION AND PREVENTION FROM FOOD BORNE ILLNESS

> Nguồn gốc: `SOP-SANITATION / FOOD BORNE ILLNESS` — KIWOOZA · F&B · BAR · All staffs · Modified by: Amber Truong · 10/06/2020.
> Bản trích xuất TOÀN VĂN (EN, 11 điểm). KHÔNG sửa file gốc.

---

1. Practice good **personal hygiene**. Associates must **wash hands** thoroughly with soap and water and use a **sanitizer** before beginning or returning to working with food or utensils.
2. **No eating, drinking or smoking** at workstations.
3. **Ready-to-eat food must not be touched with bare hands.** Use disposable gloves, tongs, or other dispensing devices.
4. **Date, label and rotate** all foods to be stored.
5. Anybody who handles food should have the **Sanitary and Hygienic Card**.
6. Use approved wiping cloths (disposable or cotton). **Terry cloth towels are not permitted** for kitchen use.
7. **Toxic and chemical materials** must be properly segregated and stored away from food, food equipment and utensils.
8. **Do not handle food if you have a cold or sore throat** — coughing and sneezing contaminate food through the air.
9. **Never handle food** if you have skin infections, sores or boils.
10. **Clean ≠ sanitary.** Clean = free of visible dirt; **sanitary = free of bacteria and microorganisms** that cause food-borne illness.
11. The bottom line: **sanitary procedures will prevent the outbreak of food-borne illness**.
