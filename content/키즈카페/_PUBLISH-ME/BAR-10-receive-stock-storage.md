## 1. Receiving stock
- Ensure that all stocks are **not damaged** before signing for them.
- **Count the stock physically** with the requisition form. Check each case for **quality and quantity**.

## 2. Filing
- Always keep a **copy of the requisition form** for our records.

## 3. Storage of stock
- **Do not store chemicals, soaps, and detergent** in the same location as food and drink.
- Use the **FIFO** (first in first out) system. Restock the **back of the cooler** with new items — this gives the appropriate time for items to reach the needed temperature.
