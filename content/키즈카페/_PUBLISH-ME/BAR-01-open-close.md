# SOP - BAR - OPEN AND CLOSE THE BAR

## OBJECTIVE
Associates understand the proper procedure to **open and close the bar** correctly and efficiently.

---

## OPENING — Procedure
- Bar set up so attendants **move the least** when preparing drinks; everything within easy reach.
- Bottles placed **according to volume**, fastest-moving in the right position.
- **Labels face the bar attendants** for better organisation & faster prep.
- Maintain bar counter cleaning daily (inside parts).
- All bottles fitted with **speed pourers**.
- Mixers within reaching distance of the bar counter.
- Garnishes within easy reach.

**Coffee machine:**
- Start coffee machine.
- **Clean the head groups 2 times** with coffee.
- Put coffee bean into the grinder.

**Machines & fridges:**
- Start all blenders/machines and fridges; switch on blenders and machine.
- Turn on all lights from **ice cream fridge, cake fridge**.
- **Unwrap** ice cream and cakes; check/top up all cakes and ice cream.

**Benches & tools:**
- Clean up all benches; make sure spoons, straws, cups, mugs, jugs, saucers are **clean and polished**.
- Organise all tools/equipment; ready frequently-used: knives, chopping boards, stirring spoons, tongs, ice cream scoops, etc.

**Fruit prep (label name + date):**
- **Lemon, strawberries:** washed & sliced → inox containers with a piece of paper towel.
- **Carrot, cucumber, watermelon, mint:** washed & cut in chunks.
- **Strawberry jam** (weekdays/weekends): strawberry cut small + brown sugar.

**Top up:** Chocolate sauce · Condensed milk · Syrup · All powders.
**Ice:** Take ice from ice maker to ice keeper.

---

## CLOSING — Procedure

**Coffee machine:**
- Turn off coffee machine.
- Clean head group with **powder (1 add) ×15 times**.
- Clean head group with **water ×5 times** again.
- Clean all baskets with powder (depends on daily coffee volume).
- Take coffee bean to a clean container.
- Clean up 2 steamers; **release all air from 2 steamers**; turn off.

**Machines & fridges:**
- Turn off blenders/machines and fridges; switch off.
- Turn off all lights from ice cream fridge, cake fridge.
- Clean all inside & outside of fridges, shelves, microwave.
- **Check temperatures of all chillers and freezers.**
- **Wrap** ice cream and cakes.

**Benches & tools:** clean benches; spoons/straws/cups/mugs/jugs/saucers clean & polished; organise tools; ready frequently-used items.

**Fruit prep (label name + date):** same as opening (lemon/strawberry sliced; carrot/cucumber/watermelon/mint chunks).
**Top up:** Chocolate sauce · Condensed milk · Syrups · All powders.
**Ice:** Take ice from ice maker to ice keeper.
