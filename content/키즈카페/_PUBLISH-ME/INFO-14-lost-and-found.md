# SOP - INFO - LOST AND FOUND REGISTER (Đồ thất lạc)

> Nguồn gốc: `SOP-INFO-15 LOST AND FOUND REGISTER` — Department: Information · Owner: wind@kiwooza.com.
> Bản trích xuất TOÀN VĂN (nguyên gốc tiếng Anh, 9 điểm). KHÔNG sửa file gốc.

---

1. An item left behind by guests either in the room or in the site area, identified by any staff and brought under the notice of site, is termed a **"Lost and Found"** item.
2. **All items** — regardless of whether valuable, non-valuable, or perishable — **must be recorded on the Lost and Found register.**
3. **Contact the guest** (call, email, zalo, kakao, …) to notify them about the lost item (if we know which customer it belongs to).
4. **Valuable items** like jewelry, mobile, wallets, laptops, iPads, etc. must be stored in a **revenue locker**.
5. **Check and compare item information carefully** before returning it.
6. Once the valuable item is collected by the owner or authorized person, **scan their ID card** for storage and import on the system.
7. **Only return the valuable item under supervisor/manager permission.**
8. **Non-valuable items** are kept for **1 month** (after this time, they are to be disposed of).
9. **Valuable items** are kept for **2 months** (after this time, they are to be given to the **police station**).
