# SOP - BAR - HOW TO OPEN AND SERVE A BOTTLE OF SPARKLING WINE

> Nguồn gốc: `SOP-OPEN/SERVE SPARKLING WINE` — KIWOOZA · F&B · BAR · All staffs · Modified by: Amber Truong · 10/06/2020.
> Bản trích xuất TOÀN VĂN (EN). KHÔNG sửa file gốc.

---

## OBJECTIVE
Help Associates understand the **sequence of serving Sparkling Wine by bottle**.

---

## 1. Prepare a bottle of sparkling wine
- **Check the wine label and bin number** against the POS printed order.
- Obtain the bottle, **put it in the wine bucket**; fill ice cubes to the rim and add **cold water**.

## 2. Present the sparkling wine
- Present horizontal, label uppermost, service cloth supporting base:
  > *"Mr./Mrs./Ms./Sir/Madam XXXX, this is the ordered sparkling wine; may I open it now?"*

## 3. Open the sparkling wine
- Remove the foil and **lift the cork's toggle** with your right hand, **twist anti-clockwise**, holding the cork with your left thumb.
- **Keep your thumb on the cork** until the wine is completely twisted open.
- Twist the cork slowly with the right hand.

## 4. Remove the cork
- Apply strength to the thumb to **prevent the cork from shooting up** when you feel it begin to rise.
- ⚠️ **No 'pop' sound** — let the cork slowly come up while keeping it firm in your hand.
- Use a dry clean service cloth; wipe the mouth of the bottle inside out.
- Place the cork upwards on the guest table beside the sparkling wine glass.

## 5. Ready to serve
- Wipe the bottle's mouth inside out with the service cloth.
- With the **label facing the guest**, pour a mouthful for the guest to taste.

## 6. Serving the sparkling wine
- After tasting, serve **clockwise**.
- **Pour 2/3** of sparkling wine in the glass.
