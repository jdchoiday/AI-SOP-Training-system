# SOP - INFO - QUY ĐỊNH MỚI DÀNH CHO CASHIER (2025)

> Nguồn gốc: `SOP- INFO - Quy trình/quy trình thanh toán mới cho Cashier 2025.docx` — Tên: "Quy định mới dành cho Cashier" · Created by: Amber · 07/2025.
> Bản trích xuất TOÀN VĂN (nguyên gốc tiếng Anh). KHÔNG sửa file gốc.

---

## 1. Professional Conduct (Tác phong)
- Wear a **uniform and name tag** at all times.
- Greet every guest with a **warm smile and polite tone**.
- Be **patient** with parents and children. Use **clear, friendly language**.

## 2. Order Accuracy & Cash Handling (Độ chính xác & xử lý tiền)
- **Confirm all orders clearly** before processing the bill check.
- **Count change aloud** and hand it over respectfully.
- **Enter every item** into the POS system — **no shortcuts**.
- Keep the **cash drawer closed** when not in use.

## 3. Payment Methods (Phương thức thanh toán)
Accept:
- ✅ **Cash (VND):** count total notes, count change **in front of customers** and hand over respectfully.
- ✅ **Bank cards:** make sure the payment **gone thru** (thành công).
- ✅ **Bank transfer:** take photo from customer's payment, ensure **"successful transaction"**.

- **Verify all payments** are successfully processed **before finalizing**.
- Hand the **final bill** to customer (total bill with payment methods).

## 4. Discounts, Refunds & Voids (Giảm giá, hoàn tiền, huỷ)
- **Only managers** may approve **refunds or cancel sales**.
- Enter **all promotions/discounts clearly** into the system.
- **Record voided or refunded orders** with reasons and **report immediately**.

## 5. End-of-Shift Routine (Cuối ca)
- **Balance drawer with POS report** (đối chiếu két với báo cáo POS).
- Use **closing checklist**:
  - Count cash
  - Report discrepancies
  - Submit shift report

## 6. Customer Relations (Quan hệ khách hàng)
- **Calmly** respond to complaints and feedback.
- Communicate **wait times** clearly and offer **polite solutions**.
- **Escalate** issues (delays, errors, complaints) to the manager **promptly**.

## 7. Security Practices (An ninh)
- **Never leave** the cash register **unattended**.
- **Report suspicious activity** or large discrepancies **immediately**.
- Handle **large bills** with discretion.
