# SOP - BAR - HOW TO OPEN AND SERVE A BOTTLE OF RED WINE

> Nguồn gốc: `SOP-OPEN/SERVE RED WINE` — KIWOOZA · F&B · BAR · All staffs · Modified by: Amber Truong · 10/06/2020.
> Bản trích xuất TOÀN VĂN (EN). KHÔNG sửa file gốc.

---

## OBJECTIVE
Help Associates understand the **sequence of Red Wine bottle service**.

---

## 1. Prepare a bottle of red wine
- **Check the wine label and bin number** against the POS printed order.
- Approach the guest's table and **present the bottle in horizontal position**.

## 2. Present wine to the guest
- Label uppermost, with a **service cloth** supporting the base, to the guest:
  > *"Mr./Mrs./Ms./Sir/Madam XXXX, this is the wine ordered, may I open it now?"*
- Place the red wine on the table.

## 3. Open the wine — cutting the foil
- Open the knife from the wine opener; knife must be **sharp, clean, not rusty**. Hold the bottle's neck firmly.
- Cut the foil **~0.5 cm down the mouth**, turn the knife **clockwise twice and anti-clockwise once**.
- **Remove the cut foil, place it in your pocket.**
- Close the knife carefully.

## 4. Pull the cork
- Pierce the cork in the middle with the opener's skewer; lift the cork upward with the right hand, left hand holding the neck firmly.
- **Wipe the bottle's mouth inside out** with the service cloth.
- Place the cork **upwards on the guest table** beside the red wine glass.

## 5. Serving the red wine
- With the **label facing the guest**, pour a mouthful quantity for the guest to **taste**.
- After tasting, serve **clockwise, ladies first**, the person who tasted **last**.
- **Pour 1/2** of red wine into the glass.
