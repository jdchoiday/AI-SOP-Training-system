# SOP - BAR - HOW TO OPERATE MINI BAR

> Nguồn gốc: `SOP-HOW TO OPERATE MINI BAR` — Saigon South Marina Club · Dept: Bar · Created by: Wind.
> Bản trích xuất TOÀN VĂN (EN). KHÔNG sửa file gốc.

---

## 1. OPENING MINI BAR
1. Turn on the **light of all chiller and display fridge**.
2. **Unwrap ice cream**.
3. Clean surface of the area.
4. Set up wooden boxes (spoon, knife, fork, ...).
5. Organize position of stuffs.

## 2. OPERATING
1. Always **check and fill up drinks** (Pepsi, Pororo, Aquafina, Sparkling water, Wine, Candy, …).
2. Follow **FIFO** (first in first out) rule.
3. Make sure available to serve customers: wooden box, plate, baby bowl, ice cream bowl, coffee spoon, ice cream spoon, sauce, sauce bowl.
4. **At weekend**, please repair ice box (fill up and bring to mini bar).

## 3. CLOSING MINI BAR
1. Turn off the **light** of all chiller and display fridge (**ONLY LIGHT**).
2. **Wrap ice cream**.
3. Clean surface of the area.
4. Set up wooden boxes (spoon, knife, fork, ...).
5. Organize position of stuffs.
6. **Fill up drinks and stuffs for the next day**.
