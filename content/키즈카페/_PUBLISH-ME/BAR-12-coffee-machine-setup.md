## OBJECTIVE
Associates know how to **setup and operate the coffee machine** properly.

---

## PROCEDURE
- Ensure it is connected with electric supply and **turn the coffee machine on**.
- Take note the unit has been **cleaned from the night before**; if not, **report to supervisor** and then proceed in cleaning.
- **Check the quantity of coffee bean.** Refill the container if required.
- **Stock up** cups, saucers, teaspoons and teapots, coffee, supplies, teas, sugars, plates and doilies for service of milk, sugar and chocolate powder.
- Make sure there are **clean towels** available for cleaning coffee area during service.
- Finally make sure set-up is **regimented and consistent** always.
- Check / top up all cakes and ice cream.
