# SOP - BAR - SERVING DRAFT BEER

> Nguồn gốc: `SOP-SERVING DRAFT BEER` — KIWOOZA · F&B · BAR · All staffs · Modified by: Amber Truong · 10/06/2020.
> Bản trích xuất TOÀN VĂN (EN). KHÔNG sửa file gốc.

---

## OBJECTIVE
Associates know all the proper steps to **prepare and serve the draft beer**.

---

## 1. Taking order
- Take guest order: *"What would you like to drink, please?"*
- If guest requests beer, ask: *"Would you prefer Draft Beer or Bottled Beer?"*
- Repeat order: *"You have ordered one XX Draft Beer, is that correct?"*
- Receive guest's order confirmation.
- Say: *"Thank you!"*
- **Post order into POS.**

## 2. Prepare the order
- Receive POS print-out.
- Take **chilled draft beer glass / mug** from refrigerator.
- Verify draft beer machine functioning properly (**CO2, Draft Beer Keg, Power On**).
- **Pour draft beer holding mug at 45 degrees sideways.**
- **Push draft beer tap backwards** to pour beer foam.
- Ensure **2–3 cm of beer foam** floating on top.
- Hand to service attendant.
- Verify order and place onto service tray.

---
*Bản trích xuất phục vụ tạo nội dung đào tạo. Giữ nguyên nguyên gốc (EN) & con số (45°, 2-3cm foam).*
