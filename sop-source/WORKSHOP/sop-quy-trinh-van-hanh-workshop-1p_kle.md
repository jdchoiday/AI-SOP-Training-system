### Overview

Display shelves

Check all the product are in the right place where it's belonged to and organize

The floor and table/chairs is clean without trash and stuffs

The products and samples must be displayed at the display area to appeal our customers attention.

The products and sample must be checked everyday about quantity and quality

If there is lack of products, put more to make sure we have enough products to sell

If there is one product too old, change the new one

Organize this area all the time by taking unrelated stuffs out.

Materials shelves

Tool shelves must be organized according to their intended use and tool classificationThe floor and table/chairs is clean without trash and stuffs

Clean the shelves weekly and submit a report to the management

The products and sample must be checked everyday about quantity and quality

If there is lack of products, put more to make sure we have enough products to sell

If there is one product too old, change the new one

Do not place unrelated items in this area

All tools and materials must be labeled with their names

Oven shelves - Cooking setup table

The oven must always be clean and odor-free

Clean it thoroughly every Monday. Do not place personal items or flammable materials around or on top of the oven (Unplug when not in use.)

The ingredient preparation table must be kept clean during weekdays and free of personal belongings

On weekends, the table must be neatly organized

All staff are required to maintain the overall cleanliness of the room

Cleaning:

Use towel to clean the table, chairs and the craft shelfs ( even every box ) everyday when staff coming to start and finish

Use water spray to clean the table if water color area on the table

The table in corner alway need clean and organize the item ( do not put personal effects )
