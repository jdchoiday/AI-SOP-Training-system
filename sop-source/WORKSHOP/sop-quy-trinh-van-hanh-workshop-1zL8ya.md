### Overview

Organize stuffs and materials:

Check all the materials are in the right place where it's belonged to and organize.

The floor and table/chairs is clean without trash and stuffs.

The products and samples must be displayed at the display area to appeal our customers attention.

The products and sample must be checked everyday about quantity and quality

If there is lack of products, put more to make sure we have enough products to sell

If there is one product too old, change the new one

Organize this area all the time by taking unrelated stuffs out.

Display shelves must be displayed and arranged every day to change content, full price list.

Products are checked for quality (moldy or damaged, no longer usable).

Charm compartments must be arranged clearly (theme charms are on top).

Compartments without charms must be decorated with something (don't leave any empty compartments).

Clean the upper shelves every week.

Send weekly reports when cabinets are cleaned.

Products must be neatly arranged, regularly checked for renewal and quantity.

Paint statues or coloring items arranged according to the type of workshop.

Products that have expired or show signs of damage, please discard.

Statues must always be arranged neatly. When customers have finished using statue painting tools, they must be washed and dried neatly on shelves.

All shelves must have sample products on display, maximum 100 slime jars per shelf.

Arrange shelves neatly and change them regularly.

Shelves are always cleaned at the beginning of the week.

Cleaning:

Use towel to clean the table, chairs and the craft shelfs ( even every box ) everyday when staff coming to start and finish.

Use water spray to clean the table if water color area on the table.

The shelves are always stocked with tools.

Slime area:

Glue bottle is always clean.

Tray must be cleaned every day.

Color bottle must be cleaned every week.

Materials must be fully operational.

Customers must not take materials to make their own. Support staff or non-department staff must monitor the workshop department's process.

Must clearly label the ingredients to avoid confusion.

Table area:

Tables and chairs are always wiped every day (wipe chairs, chair legs, seat surfaces, tables) to ensure they are always clean.

Standees, flyders, maps are always fully prepared and newly prepared every day to send to guests. Cooking programs or workshops that have taken place must be saved as documents and kept in a file (Standee,...).

The cabinets must be neatly arranged and displayed.

Locker area outside the slime hall:

Always keep each ingredient clean and tidy.

Do not leave cooking ingredients in these areas.

Expired items and ingredients are allowed to be thrown away to ensure general hygiene.

The cabinets are always arranged according to each type of workshop for easy stock checking.

Cooking class

Always maintain the setup space as shown.

Items are responsible for checking the quantity every week. If there is any loss, it must be reported to the direct manager for advice on how to resolve it.

The cart for storing raw materials is always neatly arranged. The cart is always cleaned to avoid dust and trash.

Locker

Staff are responsible for checking the hygiene every week.

Products not in plastic bags must be removed and neatly arranged.

Cooking utensils with odors must be cleaned with warm water and dried before being stored in the cabinet.

Do not leave cooking ingredients in the cabinet.

Record specific quantity on large bin.

Check stock weekly.
