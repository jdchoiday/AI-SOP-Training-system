OBJECTIVE  

Helping Associates understand the important of checking all the cooling equipment before service.   
  
  

PROCEDURE DESCRIPTION   
  

At the beginning of the shift, all cooling areas should be inspected.

Fridge should be maintained to a standard temperature so that all fruits and beverages are chilled evenly.

White wine fridges/coolers are kept at the minimum 2-8ºC. Do not over chill wines

Beverage fridges are always cooled to 2-8º C

If there is a sharp decrease or increase of the temperature in the cooling system, inform Engineering Department immediately

In case of a breakdown of the cooling system, remove wine and champagne to another cooler. Don’t wait till the Engineer repairs the cooler for wine/champagne.

Beverage items, such as cans, bottles, etc, must never block fans blowers

The Fridge/coolers systems must not be switched off it will result in damage

Vegetable/fruit must be covered or wrapped with plastic cling film before putting into the fridge

Always close the fridge quickly whenever you open it and ensure it is firmly shut
