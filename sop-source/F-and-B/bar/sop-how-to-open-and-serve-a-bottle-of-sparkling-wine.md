OBJECTIVE  

Help Associates understand the sequence of serving Sparkling Wine by bottle

PROCEDURE DESCRIPTION   
  

1\. Prepare a bottle of sparkling wine.

Check the wine label and bin number against the POS printed order.

Obtain the bottle of sparkling wine and put it in the wine bucket, fill ice cubes in the bucket until to the rim and add cold water.

2\. Present the sparkling wine

Present the bottle in horizontal position and its label uppermost with a service cloth supporting the base of the bottle to the guest and says,

“Mr./Mrs./Ms./Sir/Madam XXXX.” this is the ordered sparkling wine; may I open it now?”

3\. Open the sparkling wine.

Remove the foil and lift the sparkling wine cork’s toggle with your right hand, twist anti-clockwise, holding the cork with your left hand’s thumb.

(Keep your thumb on the cork until the sparkling wine is completely twisted open).

Twist the cork slowly with the right hand.

4\. Remove the cork.

Applied strength to the thumb to prevent the cork from shooting up when

you feel the cork is beginning to rise. (Note: no ‘pop’ sound, let the cork slowly come up from the bottle while keeping the cork firm in your hand)

Use a dry clean service cloth; wipe the mouth of the bottle inside out.

Place the cork upwards on the guest table besides the sparkling wine glass.

5\. Ready to serve.

Wipe the bottle’s mouth inside out with the service cloth.

With the label facing the guest, pour the sparkling wine consisting of a mouth full quantity for the guest to taste.

6\. Serving the sparkling wine.

After guest has tasted, serving of sparkling wine will be clockwise.

Pour 2/3 of sparkling wine in the glass.
