OBJECTIVE  
   

Associates understand the proper procedure to open and close the bar correctly and efficiently

PROCEDURE DESCRIPTION (OPENING)  
   
  

The bar set up should be completed in a manner, so bar attendants must move the least when preparing drinks. Everything should be within easy reach.

Bottles are to be placed according to the volume in the right position, with fastest moving

Labels must face the bar attendants for better organisation and faster preparation.

Maintain bar counter cleaning daily the inside parts.

All bottles are fitted with speed pourers.

Mixers should be placed within reaching distance of the bar counter.

Garnishes should be located within easy reach.

Start coffee machine

Clean the head groups 2 times with coffee

Put coffee bean into the grinder

Start all blenders/machines and fridges

Switch on blenders and machine

Turn on all lights from ice cream fridge, cake fridge

Unwap Ice cream and cakes

Check/ top up all cakes and ice cream

Clean up all benches

make sure spoons, straws, cups, mugs, jugs, saucers are clean and polished

Organise all tools and equipment

Be ready for frequently used stuff: knives, chopping boards, stirring spoons, tongs, ice cream scoops, and so on.

Cut fruits that will be used most: make sure they are labeled with name and date

Lemon, strawberries need to be washed and sliced, put into inox containers with along with a piece of paper towel

Carrot, cucumber, watermelon and mint need to washed and cut in chunk

Make strawberry jam depends on weekdays or weekends to make (strawberry cut in small piece added with brown sugar)

Top up

Chocolate sauce

Condensed milk

Syrup

All powders

Take ice from ice maker to ice keeper

PROCEDURE DESCRIPTION (CLOSING)  
   

  

Turn off coffee machine

Clean the head group with powder (1 add) for 15 times

Clean the head group with water again 5 times

Clean all the baskets with powder (depends on how much coffee make on 1 day)

take coffee bean to a clean container

Clean up 2 steamers

turn of coffee machine and

Release all air from 2 steamers

Turn off blenders/machines and fridges

Switch off blenders and machine

turn off all lights from ice cream fridge, cake fridge

Clean up all fridges/shelves/microwave

Clean all inside and outside of fridges, and shelves, microwave

Check temperatures of all chillers and freezers

Wrap Ice cream and cakes

Clean up all benches make sure spoons, straws, cups, mugs, jugs, saucers are clean and polished

Organize all tools and equipment

Be ready for frequently used stuffs: knives, chopping boards, stirring spoons, tongs, ice cream scoops, so on

Cut fruits that will be used most, and labelled them with name and dates:

Lemon, strawberries need to be washed and sliced, put into inox containers with along with a piece of paper towel

Carrot, cucumber, watermelon and mint need to washed and cut in chunk

Top up

Chocolate sauce

Condensed milk

Syrups

All powders

Take ice from ice maker to ice keeper
