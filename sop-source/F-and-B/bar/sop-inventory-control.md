OBJECTIVE  

Associates know how to do a proper inventory control PROCEDURE DESCRIPTION   

1\. All beverages must have a proper control.

2\. Service associates will conduct daily inventory.

\- Opening = closing of the day bef.

\- In – raw = stocks are bought in day

\- Pre – processing = stocks are pre – processed which are fresh fruit, mint

\- Amount of loss = (in-raw) – (pre-processing) + cancel items + spoiled stocks

\- Out – consuming = stocks are being sold in day

\- Closing = opening + (pre-processing) – (amount of loss) – (out-consuming)

3\. Cost control department will conduct weekly inventory

Ensuring all barista follow recipe cards, use measuring cups and spoons

4\. Order cancelling has to be reported to managers with reasons

5\. Bar inventory: will be checked everyday

6\. Kitchen inventory:

Daily (the day after): main ingredients with portions

Weekly (Monday): check all ingredients and stocks
