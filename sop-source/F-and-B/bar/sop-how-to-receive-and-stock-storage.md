Helping Associates understand the important of checking all the cooling equipment before service.

PROCEDURE DESCRIPTION   

1\. Receiving stock

Ensure that all of the stocks are not damaged before signing for them.

Count the stock physically with the requisition form. Check each case to ensure on the quality and quantity

2\. Filing

Always keep a copy of the requisition form for our records.

3\. Storage of stock

Do not store chemicals, soaps, and detergent in the same location as food and drink.

Use the first in first out (FIFO) system when using the stock. Restock the back of the cooler with the new items, this gives the appropriate time needed to have the items at the need temperature.
