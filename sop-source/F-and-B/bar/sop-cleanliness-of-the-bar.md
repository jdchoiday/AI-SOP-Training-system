OBJECTIVE  

  
  
  

Associates must know that cleanliness is one of the most important point of the bar

  
PROCEDURE DESCRIPTION CLEANING BAR  
   
  

Immediately after an order has been prepared, use a dry cloth/bussing towel and wipe up all the wet areas

Replace bottles and all the items used in making the beverage order in the correct location, wiping bottles if necessary

Always use a clean cloth when wiping these areas in front of the guest at the counter

Do not leave the bar service area wet and disorganized

Put knives, strainers and cutting boards, etc. back to its proper place and arrange them in an orderly manner

Clean all the equipment after use.

Pay attention to the following areas: drip trays for coffee machine, draught beer machine, bar-gun nozzle, cutting boards, pick-up areas and sinks etc.

  
PROCEDURE DESCRIPTION CLEANING FRIDGE  
   
  

Remove all items from the fridge.

Using clean cloth to wipe up all spillage

With warm soapy water, wipe out shelf base, sides, door and steel

Dry out the shelf base with a dry towel. This will ensure no water borne bacteria will survive

Wipe seals with damp cloth ensuring all mold is removed

Replace all items back in fridge

Wipe and polish thoroughly with clean towel
