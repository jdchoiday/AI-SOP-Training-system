STAFF TAKE ORDER PROCESS

Objective

\- To ensure staff get remember take order process from customer

\- All processing is smooth and no problem about f\&b - Service

RULE

CLEAN

CLOTHES NOT WRINKLED

FIT PROPERLY

GENUINE SMILE - GOOD ATTITUDE WITH CUSTOMER AND KID

UNIFORM MUST BE CLEAN, NO TEAR, SMELLED, BLEACHED

TROUSER MUST BE DARK AND NOT TOO TIGHT

HAVE SOCK DURING WORKING SHIFT

NAIL MUST BE CLEAN

CHECK BODY ODOURS BEFORE START WORKING

The posture bowing customer

" KIWOOZA XIN CHÀO "

Staffs need to say when see customer with genuine smile and good attitude

The posture of server

Give menu to customer

Need to open menu tab and give by 2 hand

Take order - take note - confirm order

Make customers feel comfortable when ordering

Focus on listening to orders and notes

Special requests beyond the ability to answer require confirmation from management

3\. Introduce our menu, new food & drink in season, promotion

Always keep genuine smile and good attitude

When giving a menu to a customer, the first thing to do:

Introduce new food\&drink

Introduce promotions

Introduce the restaurant's upcoming activities

Management will training staff take in all information to respond to customer

4\. Response customer about food and drink

Need to clearly explain to customers what ingredients are included in the food\&drink, whether there are ingredients that are allergenic or inedible.

Re-read order to confirm with customer before sending to kitchen and bar

5\. Recommend food and drink

Introduce food & drink that can be combined with each other to increase palatability and up sale

6\. Cleanup

Staff use cleaning spray to clean table, chair,baby chair,

Clean on floor if not clean enough

6\. Set up

Staff based on the amount adult and kid to prepare, including adult plates, baby plates and utensil box

Organized setup area and be cleaning everytime
