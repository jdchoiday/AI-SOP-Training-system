HOW TO TAKE ORDER ON CUKCUK SYSTEM

Objective

\- To ensure staff get remember how to take order from customer - on cukcuk system

\- Customer: Provide quality food and flawless service

\- Center: All processing is smooth and no problem about f\&b - Service

How to use cukcuk system by phone to take order

Login with staff account

Staff will have account to login in system

The staff will see the alignment and the information related as the number of people, the time to play and the price in time.( Blue button + )

Take note and send order

The staff will choose the number of tables to order, select the items the customer has selected

Note the dishes that need attention ( Press and hold the three-dot button to take notes)

Send kitchen & bar ( Blue button is below )

Change table

Press and hold the three-dot button of the table. Choose " Change table )

Choose the table match with area

. Zone A ( Bar )

. Zone B ( Playground )

. Zone C ( Fishing )

Click " OK " to save and the table will changed

Cancel order

When canceling an order, the consent of the area manager is required. Explanation of the reason for canceling the order. If not agreed, the employee must be responsible for his order

Note

Alway check order have sended to kitchen and bar

Make sure system don't have any problem

Food\&drink will be served on time
