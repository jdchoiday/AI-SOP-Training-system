1. An item left behind by guests either in the room or in the site area identified by any staff and brought under the notice of site  is termed as "Lost and Found" item.  
2. All items regardless whether it is valuable, non valuable items and perishable items must be recorded on the Lost and found register.  
3. Contact (call, email, zalo, kakao, …)  to the guest to notify the guest about the lost item (if we know which customer the item belongs to)  
4. Valuable items like jewelry, mobile, wallets, laptops, ipads etc. must be stored in a revenue locker.  
5. Check and compare item information carefully before returning it.  
6. Once the valuable item is collected by the owner or the authorized person, let's scan their ID card for storage and import on the system  
7. Only return the valuable item under supervisor/manager permission.  
8. Non-valuable items are kept for 1 months (after this time, these items are to be disposed off)  
9. Valuable items are kept for 2 months (after this time, these items are to be given to police station)
