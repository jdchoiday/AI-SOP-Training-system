1. # **PROGRAM OVERVIEW** {#program-overview}

KWZ Strong is a training program designed to enhance and develop the athleticism and physique of young children, as well as teach them how to overcome challenging tasks through various exercises and sports.

In addition to providing physical training, KWZ Strong also includes exciting sports activities such as football, basketball, mini golf, and badminton. This comprehensive approach ensures that participants receive a well-rounded athletic training program.

2. # **KIWOOZA STRONG PHILOSOPHY** {#kiwooza-strong-philosophy}

We believe that physical education is a vital component of a well-rounded education, fostering the holistic development of students. As physical education teachers, our primary goal is to inspire and empower students to lead healthy, active lifestyles while fostering their physical, social, and emotional growth.

3. # **WHAT WE EXPECT FROM YOU** {#what-we-expect-from-you}

As Kiwooza Strong Teacher, we expect that you will:

* Remember your course schedule, be fully prepared, and arrive on time (if not early) for all class meetings.  
* Endeavor to create an in-class environment that promotes learning by all is open and democratic, treats all participants evenly and fairly, and allows for social interaction.  
* Be enthusiastic about the subject area you are teaching and in your teaching of that subject.  
* Work to instill that enthusiasm in course participants but recognize that not all will necessarily adopt an enthusiastic outlook.  
* Be knowledgeable in the areas you are instructing, and be willing to admit the limits of your knowledge and experience to course participants when necessary.  
* Attempt to maintain a learner (customer) perspective in all that you do with the course, remembering that the participant is the primary reason you are there.  
* Endeavor to be an effective communicator in your class sessions, sharing your expectations and those of your participants, teaching at a level appropriate for the participants and in a manner free of biased language, actions, or activities.  
* Inform and work with Kiwooza Strong team in the event of a missed class session due to illness or emergency, scheduling and conducting a makeup session that meets the majority of the participant’s schedule.  
* Evaluate your instructional performance and the overall course, always working to improve the course and your skills as a teacher.  
* Promptly communicate to Kiwooza Strong team any change, need, concern, or problem you may have so that we may facilitate the best possible experience for you and your course participants.  
* Read, understand, and follow the policies and procedures outlined in this Teacher’s Manual.  
* Understand that nonadherence to any of the policies and procedures in this manual can result in the immediate termination of an existing teacher appointment and/or affect the possibility of future instructional opportunities.  
* Enjoy your Kiwooza Strong teaching experience.

4. # **WHAT DO CHILDREN PRACTICE IN KIWOOZA STRONG?** {#what-do-children-practice-in-kiwooza-strong?}

In KWZ Strong Program, children will become real warriors who accept our Challenges in order to achieve more valuable values for self-development. Kiwooza Strong lasts for 50 minutes. Children will adapt to different physical activities and sports under the supervision of KWZ’s teachers in every session, which helps them stimulate excitement with physical activities and sports.

**How are they designed?**  
KWZ’s teachers create challenges in Kiwooza Strong Program based on basic movements, such as running, jumping, rolling and climbing, etc. With such drills, children can develop their physique to the best, creating the foundation for their development in the future. In addition, these exercises help children generate the elements of playing specific sports, such as football and basketball.

5. # **KIDS BENEFITS AT KIWOOZA STRONG** {#kids-benefits-at-kiwooza-strong}

Allowing children to participate in indoor extracurricular activities, such as sports, helps them explore and develop skills that are considered beneficial throughout their lives. By participating in sports, children are exposed to various mental, social, emotional, physical and educational benefits. Some of these benefits are

1. **SELF DEVELOPMENT**

* Boosts Self Esteem

Several studies have suggested that taking part in sports can help with the development of your child’s self-esteem and confidence. Gestures such as a high five from a teammate, a pat on the back, or a handshake, when a match is over, help build confidence. Also, words of praise or of encouragement from the coach or players help build self-esteem.

* Social Skills

Playing sports helps children develop social skills, which would benefit them as they grow older. Playing sports teaches them about teamwork and cooperating with others and children can learn to interact with people from different ages. Also, joining sports teams helps give children a sense of belonging and gives them opportunities to make friends. With more friendship circles, it would help improve their communication skills, which will help them in their future careers and relationships.

* Self Discipline

Any sport requires athletes to maintain discipline. This discipline could be mental, physical, or tactical. To become better in any sport, you require discipline. Without self-restraint, it would be hard to succeed in any sport. Discipline helps the player achieve their goals and also reach their fullest potential.

 In sports, your child would need to follow a set of rules, take orders and accept decisions made by the coach. Good discipline is appreciated in sports, as there are penalizations for bad discipline. By listening to the coach and peers, your child would learn an important life skill that would help him throughout their career and life.

2. **PHYSICAL DEVELOPMENT**

* Weight control

Nowadays, parents have been increasingly worrying about children’s cardiovascular diseases, such as obesity, and diabetes. Physical activities in KWZ Strong Program give children the ability to keep an active life and aid them in the fight against these problems. Regular physical activities through sports will help children maintain a healthy weight and decrease the chances of illnesses like obesity and others.

* Healthy growth of bones, muscles, ligaments, and tendons

All parents want their children to grow to a normal height. Parents who are not particularly tall are often worried about their child’s height. KWZ Strong Program includes numerous physical activities and sports which help children to increase bone density, agility, and stamina. Thanks to that they could achieve the standard of global height.

* Improving children’s immune system

Participating in KWZ Strong’s activities and sports requires children to spend their whole energy and children will sweat a lot and release some noxious substances. As a result, they could boost their resistance, helping them avoid diseases, such as flu and respiratory diseases.

7. # **OUR PROGRAM CONTENTS** {#our-program-contents}

In order to ensure the class performs perfectly and smoothly, we have to prepare everything in advance, in which the teaching plan plays an important role in the success of the program. At this time, Kiwooza Strong Program is divided into 6 parts:

* **Greetings:** This is considered one of the program's most important parts. It’s released to parents that we show our thoughtfulness about caring for their kids as our close friends, which brings satisfaction to parents.

* **Warmup:** Regardless of any sport, warmup is an indispensable part, it helps children to avoid undesired injuries during their workout hours. We should remember that if children get injured, parents will not be happy and they will definitely refuse to let their children join us.

* **Basic Exercise:** Children aged 5 to 10 don't have the ability to perform high-level exercise skills. Therefore, basic exercise can help them generate the ability to perform in sports.

* **Challenges:** The main exercise is the main part of the Kiwooza Strong Program. It is also named “Challenge”, which stimulates children's excitement in physical activities. At the main exercise, teachers have to follow and supervise children very carefully in order to ensure children’s safety. 

* **Game Time:** Children tend to pay attention to games and competitions where they can beat other partners or themselves, which leads to the fact that they feel excited and interested and after that, they will join us longer. The fact is that if children are happy, parents will be happy too and they will choose us.

* **Cool Down Time:** Children tend to avoid being bored and hurtful to them. Therefore, relaxing helps them recover better after workout hours. and the most important thing is that this is a great time for us to have a direct conversation with them. We can ask them something related to the program (whether they enjoy it or not or how they feel about our program). Thanks to that we can find out the mistakes and improve our program better.
