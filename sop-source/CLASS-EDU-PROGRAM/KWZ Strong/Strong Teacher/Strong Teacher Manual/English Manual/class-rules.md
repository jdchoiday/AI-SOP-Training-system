1. Be Kind and Respectful:

* Treat your classmates, teachers, and staff with kindness and respect.

* Use positive language and gestures towards others.

2. Listen and Follow Directions:

* Pay attention when the teacher is speaking and follow their instructions.

* Raise your hand if you have a question or need help.

3. Stay Safe:

* Play safely and avoid behaviors that may cause harm to yourself or others.

* Use equipment and play areas as directed by the teacher.

4. Have Fun and Participate:

* Enjoy the activities and give your best effort.

* Participate actively and try your best, even if it's challenging.

5. Be a Good Sport:

* Play fair, share, and take turns.

* Celebrate others' successes and be a supportive teammate.

6. Use Kind Words and Actions:

* Use polite and positive words with your classmates and teachers.

* Help and encourage your peers during activities.

7. Listen to Your Body:

* Rest and ask for a break if you're feeling tired or unwell.

* Let the teacher know if you have any discomfort or pain during the activities.

8. Keep the Space Clean:

* Pick up after yourself and put the equipment back where it belongs.

* Help keep the play area tidy and free from litter.

9. Be Responsible:

* Take care of the equipment and treat it gently.

* Use equipment only for its intended purpose.

10. Enjoy Physical Activity:

* Discover the joy of movement and develop your physical skills.

* Explore different activities with a positive and curious attitude.

*Remember to explain the rules in a language and manner appropriate for their age group. Consider using visual aids or demonstrations to help them understand and remember the rules more effectively. Reinforce the rules consistently and provide gentle reminders during the class to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for all.*
