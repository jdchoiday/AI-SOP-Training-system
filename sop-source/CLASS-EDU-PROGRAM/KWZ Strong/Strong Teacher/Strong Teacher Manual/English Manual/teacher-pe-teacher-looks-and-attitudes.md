# **Looks of PE teachers:**

1.  # Professional appearance: PE teachers should present themselves professionally by wearing appropriate attire suitable for physical activity and reflective of the school's dress code policies.
2.  # Physical fitness: PE teachers should strive to maintain a level of physical fitness and demonstrate a commitment to leading an active and healthy lifestyle. This can serve as a positive role model for students and inspire them to prioritize their own physical well-being.
3.  # Safety-conscious: PE teachers should prioritize safety and demonstrate adherence to safety guidelines. They should be knowledgeable about potential risks and precautions associated with various activities and ensure that students are equipped with the necessary safety equipment.

# **Attitude of PE teachers:**

1.  # Enthusiasm and energy: PE teachers should demonstrate enthusiasm and energy for physical activity and learning. They should bring a positive attitude to each class, motivating and inspiring students to engage actively.
2.  # Adaptability and flexibility: PE teachers should be flexible and adaptable to meet the diverse needs and abilities of their students. They should be open to modifying activities or adjusting instruction based on individual differences, ensuring that all students can participate and experience success.
3.  # Encouraging and supportive: PE teachers should provide encouragement and support to students, fostering a supportive and inclusive environment. They should recognize students' efforts, celebrate achievements, and offer constructive feedback to help students improve their skills and reach their potential.
4.  # Effective communication: PE teachers should possess strong communication skills to effectively convey instructions, demonstrate techniques, and provide clear explanations. They should actively listen to students, address their questions and concerns, and facilitate positive and respectful communication among students.
5.  # Fairness and sportsmanship: PE teachers should promote fairness and sportsmanship among students. They should establish clear rules and expectations, enforce them consistently, and encourage students to exhibit good sportsmanship, fairness, and respect toward their peers.
6.  # Lifelong learning: PE teachers should demonstrate a commitment to ongoing professional development and lifelong learning. They should stay updated with current trends, research, and best practices in physical education, continuously seeking opportunities to enhance their knowledge and skills.

# *By embodying these looks and attitudes, PE teachers can create a positive and engaging learning environment, inspire students to lead active lifestyles and cultivate a love for physical activity that extends beyond the classroom.*
