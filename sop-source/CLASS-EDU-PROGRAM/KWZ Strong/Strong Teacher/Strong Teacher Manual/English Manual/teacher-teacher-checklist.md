1.  Before Class:

<!-- end list -->

  - Check the lesson plan and gather all necessary equipment and materials.
  - Ensure that the class area is clean, safe, and free of any hazards.
  - Set up any required equipment or stations.

  

1.  Attendance and Roll Call:

<!-- end list -->

  - Take attendance and note any absences or students with special considerations.
  - Verify the accuracy of the student roster.

  

1.  Safety Check:

<!-- end list -->

  - Inspect the class area and equipment for any potential safety hazards.
  - Ensure that all students are wearing appropriate attire and footwear for physical activity.

  

1.  WarmUp and Stretching:

<!-- end list -->

  - Lead a structured warmup session to prepare students for physical activity.
  - Include stretching exercises to improve flexibility and prevent injuries.

  

1.  Lesson Execution:

<!-- end list -->

  - Clearly explain the objectives and instructions for the day's activities.
  - Demonstrate proper technique and form when introducing new skills.
  - Provide individualized attention and guidance to students as needed.
  - Monitor and assess student participation and engagement.

  

1.  Equipment and Facilities:

<!-- end list -->

  - Safely distribute and collect equipment as required for different activities.
  - Ensure that equipment is used correctly and returned to its designated storage area.
  - Regularly inspect equipment for damage and report any maintenance issues.

  

1.  Behavior and Discipline:

<!-- end list -->

  - Set clear expectations for student behavior and reinforce class rules.
  - Address any disruptive behavior promptly and appropriately.
  - Encourage a positive and inclusive class environment.

  

1.  Modifications and Adaptations:

<!-- end list -->

  - Provide modifications and adaptations for students with disabilities or special needs.
  - Monitor and support the inclusion of all students in class activities.

  

1.  Hydration and Breaks:

<!-- end list -->

  - Remind students to drink water regularly and take breaks as needed.
  - Monitor and address signs of fatigue or dehydration among students.

  

1.  Closure:

<!-- end list -->

  - Conduct a cooldown session to reduce the intensity of the activity gradually.
  - Provide a summary of the lesson and highlight key takeaways.
  - Allow students to ask questions or share their reflections.

  

1.  CleanUp:

<!-- end list -->

  - Return equipment to its proper storage location.
  - Ensure that the class area is left clean and organized.

  

1.  Reflection and Evaluation:

<!-- end list -->

  - Reflect on the effectiveness of the lesson and note any adjustments for future classes.
  - Evaluate student performance and progress based on the established criteria.

  

1.  Documentation:

<!-- end list -->

  - Record any relevant observations, assessments, or incidents.
  - Maintain accurate and up-to-date records of attendance, grades, and other necessary documentation.
