# **Do's for PE Teachers:**

1.  # Do create a positive and inclusive learning environment.
2.  # Do establish clear expectations and rules for behavior.
3.  # Do plan and prepare to engage and developmentally appropriate lessons.
4.  # Do provide opportunities for student choice and autonomy.
5.  # Do promote and encourage active participation from all students.
6.  # Do provide constructive feedback and encouragement to students.
7.  # Do prioritize safety during all activities and have proper safety measures in place.
8.  # Do incorporate a variety of activities and games to cater to different interests and abilities.
9.  # Do model good sportsmanship and emphasize the importance of fair play.
10. # Do promote health and wellness education and provide resources for students to make healthy choices.

  

# **Don'ts for PE Teachers:**

1.  # Don't exclude or favor specific students; treat all students fairly and equally.
2.  # Don't neglect lesson planning and preparation; be organized and ready for each class.
3.  # Don't rely solely on traditional or repetitive activities; keep lessons varied and engaging.
4.  # Don't use negative reinforcement or embarrass students in front of their peers.
5.  # Don't push students beyond their physical limits or force participation in activities they are uncomfortable with.
6.  # Don't overlook safety protocols and guidelines; prioritize student well-being at all times.
7.  # Don't focus solely on competition; emphasize the importance of personal growth and 
8.  # Don't ignore the diverse needs and abilities of students; adapt activities to accommodate all learners.
9.  # Don't neglect professional development; continuously seek opportunities to enhance your teaching skills and knowledge.
10. # Don't underestimate the impact you can have on students' physical and mental well-being; be a positive role model and advocate for their overall health.

  

# *Remember, this list serves as a general guideline, and it's essential to consider the unique needs and circumstances of your students and school community. Adapt these do's and don'ts as necessary and always prioritize the well-being and positive growth of your students.*
