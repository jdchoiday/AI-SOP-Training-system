## What to learn?

The " " camp program will take children on a journey through 6 different "knowledge lands," equivalent to 6 weeks of camp participation:
- Exciting to explore how magical ingredients working together
- Explore the vastness of space - The Universe.
- Explore the world of art - Fine Arts.
- Explore the nature and grow a plant
- Get acquainted with the art of animation filmmaking.
- Experience different professions.

## How much?

- The tuition fee per day: 550.000 VND (3-hour class), 700.000 VND (6-hour class)
- The tuition fee per week: 2.750.000 VND (3-hour class), 3.250.000 VND (6-hour class)
- Total course (3 weeks) tuition fee (10% off): 7.425.000 VND (3-hour class), 9.450.000 VND (6-hour class)
- The tuition fee includes supplies and materials + meals and snacks

**Discount**
- Register for the full course (1 weeks or 3 weeks) before 13th December: 5% discount
- Sibblings registering (from 1 week): 5% discount
- Children registering (from 1 week) in a group of 5: 5% discount.
- Children who are currently enrolling for Tuni Tuni, Strong, Workshop class will get 5% discount

**Timeline** (3-hour class: 9am - 12pm, 6-hour class: 9am - 3pm)
- 10am - 10.30am: Welcome
- 10.30am - 12am: Themed activities
- 12am - 1pm: Lunch and group activities
- 1pm - 2pm: Cool down for rest
- 2pm - 3pm: Team building games
- 3pm - 4pm: Snack and pick up
