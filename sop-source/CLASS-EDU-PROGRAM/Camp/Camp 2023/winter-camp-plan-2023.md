**Kiwooza Winter Camp 2023**

  - Objective

<!-- end list -->

1.  To provide students a unique program through Indoor Education experience.
2.  To encourage students to develop problem solving skills.
3.  Creative inspiration for all ages
4.  Inclusive environment for children with creative space

  - Goal

<!-- end list -->

1.  Allows children to engage in various activities, build friendships, and learn new skills in a safe and supervised environment.
2.  Winter camp aims to promote personal growth, independence, teamwork, and resilience among children.
3.  Gives kids the opportunity to explore nature, engage in physical activities, and develop their creativity and problem-solving abilities.
